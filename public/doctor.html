Dr Arun Reddy M

Dr Arun Reddy M is a highly skilled neuro Surgeon specialised in performing Complex Neurological Procedures. With a vast experience and a 100% success rate in each surgery, he is considered the best neuro surgeons in Madinaguda, Hyderabad.

Dr Arun is specially trained in Cerebrovascular Surgery and Skull Base Surgery Under Prof.Yoko kato, Department of Neurosurgery, Bantane hospital, Fujita Health University, Nagoya, Japan

Dr Arun has time and again proven his dedication and expertise with his publications and continuous education and training to update his skills.
