About us

Indi Spine-Brain Neuro Care, the Best Neuro clinic in Madinaguda

Where Hope Meets Expertise

Indi Spine-Brain Neuro Care, the premier neuro clinic in Madinaguda led by Dr. Arun Reddy M, an expert neurosurgeon and spine surgeon, is dedicated to providing exceptional care and comprehensive services for all your neuro and spine healthcare needs.

Dr. Arun Reddy M is a highly skilled and experienced professional with a deep understanding of neurosurgery. With his extensive knowledge and expertise, he has successfully performed numerous surgeries, transforming the lives of his patients. Dr. Reddy is committed to delivering the highest standard of care, ensuring personalized treatment plans that prioritize patient well-being and long-term outcomes.

At Indi Spine-Brain Neuro Care, we are proud to offer state-of-the-art facilities and services. Our clinic features a cutting-edge lab equipped with the latest diagnostic technologies. This enables us to perform a wide range of tests and evaluations, ensuring accurate and timely diagnosis. With our comprehensive lab capabilities, we are able to tailor treatment plans to address each patient's specific needs.

Our in-house pharmacy is fully stocked with a wide range of neuro and spine medications, providing convenience and accessibility for our patients. Our dedicated team of pharmacists ensures that patients receive the prescribed medications promptly, with a strong emphasis on patient safety and adherence to the highest quality standards.
